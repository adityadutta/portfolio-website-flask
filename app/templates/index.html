About me

I was born in New Delhi, India. I have been programming since the age 12. I was excellent in solving puzzles with my general instincts since I was 3 years old. With time passing by, I decided to study Science in high school to finally pursue a career in the gaming industry.

As a young boy, I was always fascinated by games and whenever got a chance, I used to enjoy playing various games available in video parlours in my neighbourhood market or during a local tour with my parents. Thereafter, I started spending hours exploring new games in my computer. The activity never lost its zest and as I grew up, I knew that I wished to become a game developer and started working towards my dream of developing games for the best names in the world. I was confident about my choice and knew that it is the best option as per my interest and ability.
